Malayalam (/mæləˈjɑːləm/; മലയാളം) is a Dravidian language spoken in India, predominantly in the state of Kerala. It is one of the 22 scheduled languages of India and was designated as a Classical Language in India in 2013.
The Malayalam language has 56 letters

These letters may be classified as:

  • 15 vowels
  • 36 consonants
  • other symbols
